Commercial Portfolio

Welcome to my commercial portfolio.

Here you can explore my experience, achievements, and performance in the sales field—from my early days in direct customer sales, to managing physical stores and e-commerce platforms, and most recently, my role as a Business Executive in a tech company.

Commercial

Learning Stage

My first job was as a salesperson at Almacenes ONLY when I was 15 years old. I worked during special seasons such as Christmas and Father’s Day at the Chapinero store. This was my first experience in the commercial world, earning a base salary plus commission.

Local Business Venture

Years later, I co-founded a jewelry business called D.C. Accesorios, which operated both a physical store and an e-commerce platform. I was involved in supplier relations, customer service, web-based sales, and overall management. Although the project is currently inactive, there are plans to relaunch it soon.

PSL

My most recent experience was as a Business Executive at a tech company, where I specialized in selling cloud-based software. My responsibilities included delivering product demos, lead follow-ups, CRM management, preparing quotes, and closing deals. Most sales were made directly to businesses through their accountants or financial managers.
